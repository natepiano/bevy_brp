Clicks a mouse button in Bevy apps via bevy_brp_extras. Performs a simple click (press and immediate release) with default 100ms timing.

Button options: Left, Right, Middle, Back, Forward

Examples:
```json
{"button": "Left"}              // Click left button
{"button": "Right"}             // Click right button
{"button": "Middle"}            // Click middle button (wheel)
```

Prerequisites: bevy_brp_extras dependency and BrpExtrasPlugin registered.
